Institute of Business Management (IoBM)

Established in 1995, IoBM is one of Pakistan’s premier business schools, offering quality education in business, computer science, and engineering. Known for its commitment to academic excellence and industry collaboration, IoBM nurtures future leaders and innovators.

Technova Competition

Technova is the ultimate coding showdown hosted by IoBM that brings together talented coders to solve challenging real-world problems. It aims to promote innovation, collaboration, and growth in the tech community through engaging modules and exciting awards.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to foster a vibrant tech ecosystem by empowering students with hands-on experience and meaningful competition. Technova encourages continuous learning and networking among Pakistan’s brightest young programmers.
